About ClearView

ClearView was founded by two passionate teens from Aotea College with a vision to redefine window cleaning. We focus on delivering exceptional service, from residential to commercial spaces, ensuring every pane is spotless and every customer satisfied.

Our approach combines dedication, attention to detail, and a commitment to excellence. We believe in making the window cleaning process seamless, hassle-free, and above all, reliable. Whether it’s a one-time clean or regular maintenance, we’re here to ensure your view is always clear.
